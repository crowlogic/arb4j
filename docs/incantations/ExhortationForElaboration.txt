Exhortation for Elaboration

Generate an exposition. Provide complete exegesis, explication, and
decipherment of the subject. Supply elucidation and clarification of
every non-trivial step. Offer interpretation where ambiguity exists,
illumination where assumptions are buried, and expounding on
consequences. Ground the explanation through exemplification and
illustration. Where complexity is artificial, perform
demythologization and simplification. Deliver commentary that is
precise and explanation that is total. The result should constitute
full enlightenment on the topic — nothing implicit, nothing
hand-waved, nothing skipped.
