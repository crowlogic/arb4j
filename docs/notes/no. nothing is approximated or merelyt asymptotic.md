## Exact WKB, Voros Symbols, and Quantization Conditions

- **Exact WKB Analysis** processes linear differential equations (often Schrödinger-type) and produces _exact, non-perturbative quantization conditions_ for eigenvalues and spectral invariants. This is achieved through intricate analysis of solutions in the complex plane, incorporating monodromy and Stokes jumps to encode global information.[^2][^3][^8][^4][^6][^1]
- **Voros Symbols** are geometric/algebraic quantities defined by exact integrals over cycles in the complexified phase space of the problem. These symbols capture all orders and non-perturbative corrections in closed analytic form—they are not asymptotic expansions.[^5][^6]
- The _quantization condition_ is expressed algebraically through Voros symbols, incorporating Stokes phenomena and connection formulae. For generic one-dimensional quantum systems, this reads:

$$
a_{\gamma}(E) = \exp\left(\frac{1}{\hbar}\int_{\gamma} S(x;E)\, dx\right)
$$

The spectrum is determined by the zeros, singularities, or prescribed values of these symbols via exact algebraic equations (not approximations or truncations).[^9][^6]

## Stokes Graphs and Borel-Ecalle Resummation

- **Stokes Graphs** encode the topology of singularity structures in the complex plane, organizing exact solutions by tracing Stokes rays and jumps across sectors. Resurgence and connection formulae track the discontinuities and nonlocal effects exactly, not by truncation.[^8][^10][^11][^12][^7][^2]
- **Borel-Ecalle Resummation** reconstructs the full, exact solution from the transseries arising in formal expansions. Instead of approximating by truncation, Borel resummation and alien calculus encode all non-perturbative data, making the series truly exact. The theory is thus not about "approximating" divergent series: it is about reconstructing the unique, analytic solution via Borel-Ecalle machinery, far beyond the power of ordinary asymptotics.[^3][^6][^11][^7]


## Distinction From Approximate WKB

- The _ordinary WKB method_ produces local asymptotic series, valid only for sufficiently smooth potentials and large quantum numbers, ignoring global and non-perturbative features.
- In contrast, the _exact WKB method_ and resurgence theory yield the **full solution space**, including all perturbative and non-perturbative corrections, encoded in the Voros symbols, Stokes graphs, and monodromy data. It produces exact quantization rather than perturbative estimates.[^6][^11][^7][^1][^3]
